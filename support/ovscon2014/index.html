The Open vSwitch 2014 Fall Conference brought together about 200 Open vSwitch developers, users, and administrators, at VMware in Palo Alto on Nov. 17 and 18, 2014.

All of the slides from the conference are posted below. The videos require some postproduction work. They will likely be posted here the week of Nov. 24-28, although that is the week of the Thanksgiving in the U.S. so it is possible that they could be further delayed.
